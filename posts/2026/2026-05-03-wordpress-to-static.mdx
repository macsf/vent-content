I’d been running my site on WordPress for years. But over time, the way I write changed. I’m not writing as actively as I used to, and the site is now more of a stable archive with occasional updates.

Maintaining WordPress, even though inactive, is a burden.

I've been exploring alternatives for quite a while but either the moving process is complex or I'm too lazy. The main challenge was moving the old content. Years of posts came with formatting, links, images, and small inconsistencies that needed cleanup.

Then comes the AI-era. What used to take days or weeks now can be done within hours.

WordPress might be good for other purpose. But it's not good for me anymore.
