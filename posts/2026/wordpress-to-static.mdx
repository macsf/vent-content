I'd been running my site on WordPress for years. But over time, the way I use the site changed. Still have things to vent about, but I’m not writing as actively as I used to, and the site is now more of a stable archive with occasional updates.

Maintaining WordPress, even for an inactive site, became a burden.

I've been exploring alternatives for quite a while, but either the moving process is complex, or was too lazy to start. The main challenge was moving the old content. Years of posts came with formatting, links, images, and small inconsistencies that needed cleanup.

Then came the AI-era. What used to take days or weeks could have been done within hours. So I decided to take the plunge this morning. ... And now it's done.

WordPress might be good for other purposes. But it's not good for me anymore.
